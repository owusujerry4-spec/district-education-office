Welcome to Offinso North Education Directorate

Established in 2008, we are committed to enhancing educational standards across 93 Basic schools, 4 Senior High Schools, 2 TVET Schools and 1 National Ambulance Training School in the Offinso North District. Explore our initiatives, resources, and achievements to learn more about our dedication to quality education.
