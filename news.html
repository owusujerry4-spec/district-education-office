Brief History of the Directorate

Established in 2008, the Offinso North Education Directorate has been dedicated to improving educational standards in the district, overseeing 93 Basic schools, 4 Senior High Schools and 2 TVET Schools and as well implementing innovative teaching programs.

  • Improved GALOP program implementation in 6 schools in the district
